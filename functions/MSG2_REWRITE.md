## Why this works (vs the Yerba version)

1. **Pays off the CTA promise instantly.** They commented to GET a remedy — they get it in full, first line. Trust intact when the pitch arrives 3 seconds later.
2. **Congruent with the page.** Same product (notebook), same voice (Wei), same remedies they'll see on the landing page. No scoop-supplement whiplash.
3. **The freebie IS the proof.** A real recipe with real prep instructions signals the notebook is real. The Yerba message asked for trust; this one earns it.
4. **Savings on the button, never the price.** "SAVE 70%" tells them it's a paid product (kills the free-expectation bounce) while anchoring them on the discount, not the cost. The only number they carry to checkout is what they save.
